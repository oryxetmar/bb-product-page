Our Beans

The Process: From Little Seed to Steaming Latte

Our Product

With such a strong dedication the entire process of our product, we can confidently say we strive for imperfect perfecttion in every bag. We know that each cup of coffee starts with the time consuming, tedious task of hand picking raw coffee cherries. We know each cherry needs to be weighed and ssorted through before even hitting out roasting process to ensure the proper flavor profile. This is a work of love before anything else. And that dedication, we believe, shows in the beans that we proudly produce.

We are very passionate about involvment in each step of our process. This means that we have a hands on approach to the grinding and barista training at each of our cafes. We do not want our dedication to end at the harvesting, roasting or packaging. Our product is just as important in the beginning stages as it is behind the counter at our cafes. We host bi-monthly barista training sessions at every location. Fun, interractive and team building opportunites to learn from each other and educate on everything from flavor profiles to nut mylk steaming.

What We Do & Why

The more oil preserved within the bean, the more exotic the end profile will be. Fire roasting beans retains a much higher humidity within the process. This not only preserves the beans natural flavor but also the lipids of each bean. Although this is a slower roasting process, it ensures our coffee is lower in acidity and higher in body. Try our espresso or pour-over coffees to taste the, smooth crema, fuller flavor, and a delicate finish.
